# Ophthalmos Voice Agent Proposal

A lead qualification and proposal drafting session on 2026-04-29 for Ophthalmos (ophthalmiccentre.com), a major Cyprus ophthalmology institute run by Dr. Tasos Georgiou with 70+ staff and 100K+ patients, GESY-affiliated. The client needs an AI voice agent handling 200-300 calls/day with Cypriot Greek dialect support. This session established healthcare voice AI pricing benchmarks and identified Cypriot Greek as a significant technical scope multiplier.

## Key Points

- **Healthcare voice AI pricing benchmarks:** General market rates are $15K-35K setup, healthcare-specific can reach $75K. Qualia's pricing (€11K-15K setup, €600/month retainer) is competitive but on the low end for the healthcare vertical — room to increase for future healthcare clients
- **Cypriot Greek is significantly harder than standard Greek for voice AI:** The dialect has unique phonological features, vocabulary, and prosody that standard Greek ASR/TTS models handle poorly. This is a key scope/pricing factor for any Cyprus voice agent project
- **200-300 calls/day volume:** This is a high-volume use case that requires telephony infrastructure (Retell AI + Telnyx) rather than a website widget (ElevenLabs)
- **Intake form pattern:** Built at qualiasolutions.net/ophthalmos (access code: 516278) with budget tiers (under €5K / €5-10K / €10-25K / €25K+), timeline limited to May/June/July 2026, free demo as value-add
- **Prestige client dynamics:** Ophthalmos is not price-shopping — quality and reputation matter more than cost. Qualia's pricing should reflect the healthcare premium

## Details

The lead came from N. Neokleous (likely operations/admin at Ophthalmos). Research revealed ophthalmiccentre.com is a major Cypriot ophthalmology institute: multiple departments, 70+ medical and support staff, 100K+ patients over their history, affiliated with GESY (Cyprus's national health system), and led by Dr. Tasos Georgiou. This is a prestige client — the kind of engagement that validates Qualia's voice AI capabilities for the healthcare vertical.

The Cypriot Greek dialect challenge deserves special attention. Standard Greek ASR (automatic speech recognition) and TTS (text-to-speech) models are trained primarily on standard modern Greek, which differs from the Cypriot dialect in several ways: consonant and vowel shifts, loanwords from Turkish and English that don't appear in standard Greek corpora, distinct prosodic patterns, and code-switching between Cypriot and standard Greek within the same conversation. For a medical voice agent handling 200-300 calls daily, misrecognition of drug names, procedures, or appointment details due to dialect mismatch is a patient safety concern, not just a UX issue. This elevates the dialect requirement from a "nice to have" to a critical technical scope factor that justifies premium pricing.

The pricing assessment compared Qualia's proposed rates (€11K-15K setup + €600/month retainer including 2,500 minutes, overage per-minute) against market benchmarks. For general-purpose voice AI agents, the market ranges $15K-35K. Healthcare-specific voice AI with regulatory compliance (GDPR, medical data handling), EHR integration potential, and bilingual support can command $50K-75K. Qualia's pricing is fair for a Cyprus-market engagement but leaves margin on the table compared to US/EU healthcare voice AI providers. The key cost drivers are: Cypriot dialect training/fine-tuning, medical context safety requirements, GDPR compliance for patient call data, potential EHR integration, and bilingual (Greek/English) handling.

The intake form at qualiasolutions.net/ophthalmos follows the pre-proposal pattern used for other Qualia clients (similar to the Fotini questionnaire at qualiasolutions.net/fotini). Budget tiers are structured to qualify the lead early: under €5K (likely mismatch), €5-10K (feasible but constrained), €10-25K (sweet spot), €25K+ (full scope). Timeline options were limited to May/June/July 2026 to create urgency without being artificially restrictive. A free demo was positioned as the primary call-to-action to lower the barrier to engagement.

A Zoho Books estimate (QT-1162) was saved as draft but not sent — the delivery method is via email with the estimate PDF attached. The email draft in Zoho Mail needs the intake form URL pasted into the empty link placeholder before sending.
